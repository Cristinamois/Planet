Mars, often nicknamed the "Red Planet" due to its distinctive color, is a rocky planet with arid deserts, craters, extinct volcanoes, and polar ice caps. Exploration missions have revealed evidence of its tumultuous geological past, including hints suggesting the presence of past liquid water on its surface.
