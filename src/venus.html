OVERVIEW

Venus, often referred to as Earth's "sister planet" due to its similar size and composition, is a planet shrouded in a thick atmosphere composed mainly of carbon dioxide and sulfuric acid. Its dense atmosphere creates intense greenhouse effect, making Venus the hottest planet in the solar system despite being farther from the Sun.
